→ Open: app/lib/api.ts
→ Find: const exchangeRate = 280;
→ Change 280 to current USD-PKR rate

Change Refresh Time (currently 5 minutes):
→ Open: app/page.tsx, app/prices/page.tsx, app/watchlist/page.tsx
→ Find: 5 * 60 * 1000
→ Change to: 1 * 60 * 1000 (1 minute) or 30 * 1000 (30 seconds)
